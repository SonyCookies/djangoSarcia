Moderate Flood Alert - Northville

Issued 1 day ago

Northville has moderate flood levels. Residents are advised to stay alert and prepare for potential evacuation.

Flood safety tips:

  • Keep important documents safe and dry.
  • Have an emergency kit ready with food, water, and medications.
  • Stay informed about weather conditions and emergency alerts.

If conditions worsen, be prepared to evacuate to higher ground.
